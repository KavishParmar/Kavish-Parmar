## If you're still seeing just plain text:

Please try these steps:

1. **Hard refresh the page**: Press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)

2. **Clear browser cache**: 
   - Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete`
   - Select "Cached images and files"
   - Click Clear

3. **Open in incognito/private window**: Right-click the link and choose "Open in Incognito Window"

4. **Check browser console**: Press `F12` and look for any red error messages

Once you refresh, you should see a fully styled, professional portfolio website with purple accents, proper spacing, hover effects, and all visual elements.
